Yoga

Yoga is a physical, mental, and spiritual practice that originated in ancient India over 5,000 years ago. The word "yoga" comes from the Sanskrit word "yuj" meaning "to unite" or "to join"

Benefits of Yoga Reduces Stress and Anxiety: Yoga helps calm the mind and reduce stress hormones. Improves Flexibility and Balance: Yoga increases flexibility, balance, and range of motion. Strengthens Muscles: Yoga builds strength, especially in the core, arms, and legs. Improves Posture: Yoga helps maintain good posture and reduces back pain. Enhances Cardiovascular Health: Yoga lowers blood pressure, improves circulation, and reduces risk of heart disease.
